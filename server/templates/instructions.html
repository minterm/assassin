How to Play Bluetooth Assassin

What do I need?

To play this game, all you need is an Android phone with Bluetooth, GPS, an Internet connection, and some friends.

Gameplay Recommendations

Start by creating a new game. Enter in the names of all the players. Once the game has been created, all players must open the Android app on their phones and join the game, using their names and the game ID shown on the web page. Now the game has been created.

Inside the app, ensure that each phone has Bluetooth turned on and discovery enabled. Displayed at the top is their target's Bluetooth MAC address. When they are in range of their target, the assassinate button will be used to attempt to assassinate the target. Deaths are updated automatically at 20 second intervals, and new targets are acquired after each assassination. Locations are also periodically updated. Do you have what it takes to be the last one standing?
